Budgie

very frendly small parrots
Scientific name: Melopsittacus undulatus
Habitat: birds found in open habitats, primarily in scrublands, open woodlands, and grasslands of Australia
Feed: Budgies eat a variety of seeds (grass seeds), fruits, berries and vegetation in the wild. They feed on or near the ground.
Fun fact: Budgies are the most popular type of pet bird. In fact, 45% of pet birds in the United States are budgies.

Budgies are wonderful companions for anyone, from children to adults, families to empty-nesters
